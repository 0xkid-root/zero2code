## 🎨 Design System

### Color Palette
- **Primary**: `#FF6B35` (Orange) - Professional, energetic
- **Background**: `#FAF6F0` (Cream) - Warm, welcoming
- **Foreground**: `#2D2D2D` (Dark Gray) - Readable, elegant
- **Secondary**: `#F5E6D3` (Light Beige) - Accent backgrounds
- **Muted**: `#E8DCC8` (Warm Gray) - Borders, secondary

### Typography
- **Font**: Geist (Google Font) - Modern, professional
- **Headings**: Up to 48px, bold weight
- **Body**: 16px with 1.6 line-height
- **Contrast**: WCAG AA compliant (4.5:1+)

### Responsive Design
- Mobile-first approach
- 5 breakpoints (sm, md, lg, xl, 2xl)
- Touch-friendly targets (48px minimum)
- Flexible layouts (Flexbox primary)
